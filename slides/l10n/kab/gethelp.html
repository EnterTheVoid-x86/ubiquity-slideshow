Amuddu n ufus d tallelt

Tasemlit tunṣibt tegber ddeqs n yiḥricen imugan ɣef esterOS. Yella deg srid neɣ s tignit n tallalt deg Dock.

Deg Suter esterOS tzemreḍ ad testeqsiḍ daɣen ad tnadiḍ ddeqs n tegrummiwin n yisteqsiyen i yesɛan yakan tiririyin. Tallalt s tutlayt-ik/im izmer ad tettunefk deg Tarbaɛt-ik/im Tamɣiwent Tadigant.

I yisewwaṛen ɣer tiɣbula i iwulmen, rzu ma ulacaɣilif ɣer temqiwent n tallalt neɣ tallalt s tedrimt.
